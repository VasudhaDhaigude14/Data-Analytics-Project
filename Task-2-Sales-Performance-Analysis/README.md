# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis

## 📌 Objective
Analyze sales performance across different regions, product categories, and time periods to identify business trends, top-performing products, and opportunities for improvement.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

## 📂 Dataset
Kaggle Superstore Sales Dataset

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bhanupratapbiswas/superstore-sales

## ✅ Tasks Completed
- Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
- Sales KPI Calculation
- Revenue Analysis
- Average Order Value Analysis
- Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
- Regional Sales Performance
- Category-wise Sales Analysis
- Top Performing Products Visualization
- Business Insights & Recommendations

## 📈 Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Average Order Value
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Region
- Sales by Category
- Top Products

## 📊 Visualizations
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Region
- Sales by Category
- Top 10 Products

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing on high-performing regions.
- Improve inventory for best-selling products.
- Promote low-performing categories.
- Launch seasonal sales campaigns.
- Increase average order value using product bundles.
